About Team 2584

Team 2584 is a team made and run by a fun-loving and hard working group.Together, we are learning valuable engineering skills such as CAD, machining, programming, and the importance of strong engineering documentation. These experiences are preparing us for the opportunities that await us at the college level and beyond.
