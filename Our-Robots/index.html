Red Rover

Red Rover is our 2019 Deep Space robot. It utilizes a swing arm and free rotating wrist in order to score cargo and hatches. It has a mechanism that allows it to climb to hab level 3 as well as a Limelight for lining up with scoring objects. It also utilizes a wes coast drive.

Inky

Inky was our roboot for the 2018 FRC game Power Up. Inky featured a swing arm with a piston-actuated wrist for scoring Power Cubes on the switch and the scale. Inky utilized a west coast drive as well as a Pigeon IMU for driving.
